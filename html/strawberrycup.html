Strawberry Cup

Strawberry cup ice cream is a frozen dessert made with strawberries or strawberry flavoring, cream, eggs, sugar, and vanilla. It's usually pink or light red in color. Here's some information about strawberry cup ice cream Strawberry ice cream is a flavor of ice cream made with strawberry or strawberry flavoring. It is made by blending in fresh strawberries or strawberry flavoring with the eggs, cream, vanilla, and sugar used to make ice cream.
