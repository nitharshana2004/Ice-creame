Chocolate Cone

Each cone contains approximately 30g of Cadburys Drinking Chocolate and is topped with crushed Cadburys Flake. It is then finished off with a handful of mini marshmallows for you to pop on top once your hot chocolate is made. Ice cream cones are made from wheat flour, corn flour, and sugar. The amount of sugar in the cone is a key factor in distinguishing different types of cones.
