Cup ice-Creame

Ice cream cups are a popular way to serve ice cream, and they can be made from a variety of materials including plastic, paper, bamboo, kraft, and cardboard. Some ice cream cups are coated with bioplastic to prevent leaking and can be composted. if most of your customers don't dine-in, paper ice cream cups are probably your best option because they're better for take-out and to-go customers.
